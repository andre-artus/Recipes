# Mushroom Risotto

## Ingredients
* 35g Butter
* 35g Olive Oil
* 120g Onions (fine)
* 500g Mushrooms
* 1-2  Garlic (minced)
* 200g rice
* thyme
* 1 tsp soy
* 125ml white wine (~1/2 cup)
* 650g Stock

* salt & pepper
* parsley
* parmesan
 

## Directions

* Saute dry ingredients
* Add wine
* add stock
* pressure cook 5 min
* add herbs and cheese
