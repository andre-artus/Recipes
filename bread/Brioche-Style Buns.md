# Brioche-Style Buns — Half Batch (1 Egg)

**Makes:** 4 medium buns (~120 g each)

## Ingredients

* 250 g strong white bread flour
* 25 g caster sugar
* 5 g fine sea salt
* 3.5 g instant yeast (or 10.5 g fresh yeast)
* 125 ml whole milk, lukewarm (~30 °C)
* 1 large egg (~50 g) at room temp
* 40 g unsalted butter, very soft
* Egg wash: 1 egg + 7–8 ml milk (optional)
* Optional: sesame seeds

## Method

Same as before, just shorter knead times because of the smaller batch:

1. **Mix** dry ingredients.
2. **Add** milk, egg, and yeast mixture, knead until smooth.
3. **Add** butter in pieces, knead until glossy.
4. **First rise**: 1–2 hrs until doubled.
5. **Shape** into 4 balls, place on tray.
6. **Second rise**: 45–60 min until puffy.
7. **Bake** at 190 °C (fan 170 °C) for 15–16 min until golden.
