# Chocolate Cake with Oil

## Ingredients

* 225g chocolate
* 200g caster sugar
* 1 Tbsp golden syrup
* 250ml double cream
* 1tsp vanilla extract 



### Method
1. Add all except vanila to pot
2. Heat and stir on low to disolve sugar
3. Simmer gently until 95C (soft ball) 3-4 mins
4. Add vanilla at 90C
