# Chicken Thighs And Drums


## Ingredients

### Marinade - Base
* Salt  3 finger pinch / 100g
* Pepper 1 - 2 tsp
* Coriander 2 tsp
* Cumin 1/2 - 1 tsp
* Nutmeg    1/8 tsp

* Oregano   1/4 cup
* Garlic 2-3 cloves
* Lemon zest
 
* Olive Oil     1/4 - 1/2 cup
 

### Marinade - Lemon & Herb
* Lemon 1/2 - 1 cup
* Basil 1/4 cup
* Tarragon 1/8 cup 
* Rosemary  1/8 cup

* Thyme 1 tsp
* Clove 1/8 tsp


### Marinade - Peri Peri

* Paprika 1 Tbsp 
* Chilli (taste)
* Rosemary  1/8 cup
* Nutmeg    1/8 tsp
* Lemon or red vinegar 1/4 - 1/2 cup



### Other

* 1.5 Kg Chicken Thighs/Legs
* White wine 
* Stock
* Flour (for thickening)
* 

## Directions

1. Mix marinade
2. Marinade chicken
3. Roast in the oven
    * 30 min @ 200 C (FF) | 210 C (N)
    * 20 min @ 160 C (FF) | 180 C (N)

### Note 
Once did this during loadshedding and got cut short by 15 min, left the chicken to rest in the hot oven for that time, came out great.
