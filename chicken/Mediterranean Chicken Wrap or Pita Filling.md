# Mediterranean Chicken Wrap or Pita Filling

## Ingredients

* 500g (1 lb) boneless chicken thighs or breasts

**For the marinade:**

* 3 tbsp olive oil
* Juice of 1 lemon
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 1½ tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp smoked paprika (or regular)
* 1 tsp ground coriander
* ½ tsp turmeric
* ½ tsp cinnamon (optional but great depth)
* ½ tsp chili flakes or cayenne (optional for heat)
* Salt & black pepper to taste
* 2 tbsp plain yogurt (optional — makes it tender and tangy)

**For serving:**

* Pita bread or lavash wraps
* Sliced cucumber and tomato
* Thinly sliced red onion (soaked briefly in lemon juice for mildness)
* Lettuce or shredded cabbage
* Fresh herbs (parsley, mint, or cilantro)

**Sauce options:**

* **Garlic yogurt sauce:** Mix ½ cup yogurt + 1 clove minced garlic + squeeze of lemon + salt + drizzle of olive oil.
* **Tahini sauce:** Mix ¼ cup tahini + 2 tbsp lemon juice + 1 clove garlic + water to thin + pinch of salt.
* **Hummus** also works perfectly as a base layer.

---

## Instructions

1. **Marinate the chicken:**
   Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken and toss to coat. Marinate at least 30 minutes (better 2–6 hours in the fridge).

2. **Cook the chicken:**

   * Grill, pan-sear, or bake (200°C / 400°F for 20–25 min).
   * Aim for a little char for flavor.
   * Rest 5 minutes, then slice thinly.

3. **Assemble:**

   * Warm the pita or wrap.
   * Spread yogurt or tahini sauce.
   * Add chicken, vegetables, and herbs.
   * Drizzle extra sauce or a little olive oil.
   * Roll or fold and enjoy!

---

#### **Variations**

* **Shawarma-style:** Add ½ tsp allspice and a pinch of ground cloves to the marinade.
* **Greek-style:** Add oregano instead of turmeric and serve with tzatziki.
* **Spicy harissa version:** Mix 1 tbsp harissa paste into the marinade.
