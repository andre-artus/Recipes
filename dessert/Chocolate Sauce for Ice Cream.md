# Chocolate Sauce for Ice Cream

## Ingredients
- 250 ml (1 c) sugar  
- 60 ml (4 T) cocoa  
- Pinch cream of tartar  
- Pinch salt  
- 125 ml (½ c) water  
- 15 ml (1 T) butter  
- 2.5 ml (½ t) vanilla essence  

## Instructions
1. Mix sugar and cocoa, romoving lumps.
2. Add CoT, salt, and water.
2. Boil for 5 min.
2. Stir in the butter and cool slightly.
3. Add the vanilla essence.
4. Serve hot with ice cream.
