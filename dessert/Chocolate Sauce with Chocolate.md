# Chocolate Sauce with Chocolate

## Ingredients
- 100 ml milk
- 100 ml heavy cream
- 1/2 Tbsp sugar
- Pinch salt  
- 100 g chocolate chips
- 1/4 tsp vanilla essence

## Instructions
1. Combine the first four ingredients and (medium) heat until almost boiling.
2. Take of the heat and stir in the chocolate and vanilla.
