# Cookies ~ Margie Botha ~

### Ingredients

* 220 g (1 Cup) Soft Butter
* 230 g (1 Cup) Sugar
* 2 Eggs
* 1.5 tsp. Vanilla Essence
* 500g (3 Cups) Self Raising Flour
* 1/2 Cup chopped Nuts (optional)

### Instructions
1. Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla essence.
2. Stir in flour and nuts.
3. Divide into 3 equal parts and shape into rolls.
4. Wrap in plastic and chill for 4 hours or store in deep-freezer until required.
5. Cut into slices and press down with wet fork. 
6. Sprinkle vermicelli, hundreds and thousands etc. on each cookie as desired.
7. Bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes

### Variation:
Can use dough to make tartlets.
