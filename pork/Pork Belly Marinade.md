# Pork Belly Marinade

## Ingredients
* 1 stick cinnamon
* 1 tsp chilli flakes or 1 chopped chilli
* 6 whole cloves
* 1 tsp fennel seeds
* 1 tsp cumin
* five spice
* pepper corns
* 1/4 cup brown sugar
* 2 cloves garlic sliced
* 3cm minced ginger
* 1 Tbsp olive oil
* 1/2 tsp sesame oil
* 2 Tbsp soy sauce
* 1/4 cup sherry or Chinese cooking wine
* 1 + 1/2 cup chicken stock
* 1 cup water


### Rub for rind
* 2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp olive oil



## Directions

* Saute dry ingredients
* Add wine
* add stock
* pressure cook 5 min
* add herbs and cheese
