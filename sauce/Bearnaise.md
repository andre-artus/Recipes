# Béarnaise sauce

### For 4
* 250 g of unsalted butter (for the clarified butter)
* 4 egg yolks mixed 1 table spoon of water.
* 3 shallots finely chopped
* 4 Tbsp chopped tarragon
* 2 Tbsp chopped chervil
* 200 ml of white wine
* 100 ml of tarragon vinegar
* 2 tsp of freshly cracked black pepper
* Salt


### For 2
* 2 Tbsp of good quality dry white wine
* 3 Tbsp of tarragon vinegar 
* 2 egg yolks
* 2 Tbsps of cold water
* 100 grams of clarified butter
* 15 grams of finely chopped French shallot ( Aprox 1 small shallot)
* 2 Tbsp of freshly chopped tarragon (1 to start with then add the other one at the end)
* 1 Tbsp of freshly chopped chervil (1 to start with then add the other 1 at the end)
* 1 tsp of black peppercorn (roughly crushed)
* a pinch salt for the seasoning
