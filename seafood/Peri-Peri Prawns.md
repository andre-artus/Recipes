# Peri Peri Prawns

You may need to double the recipe. 

## Ingredients
* salt (1/8 tsp / 300g)
* black pepper
* 1 to 2 chopped chillis
* 1/2 tsp Paprika
* 1/2 tsp Coriander
* 1 (large) Crushed garlic
* 1/2 Lime or lemon juice
* 2 Tbsp olive oil



# Method
Mix & Marinate prawns 20-30 minutes (too long and the acid cooks the prawns, makling it mealy).
