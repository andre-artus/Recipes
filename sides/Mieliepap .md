# Mieliepap 

## Ingredients
* 5 - 6 cups water
* 1 Tbsp Butter or Olive Oil
* 1 tsp soy or salt
* thyme
* 1 cup mieliemeel

## Ingredients
### Stywe pap
* 750 ml maize meal
* 500ml boiling water
* 1 teaspoon salt

### Slap pap
* 500 ml maize meal
* 1 litre boiling water
* 1 teaspoon salt

### Krummel pap
* 625 ml maize meal
* 450 ml boiling water
* 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

* Boil water with butter, salt etc.
* Stir flour into boiling water
* Bring down to medium and simmer for 30-45 min (med) to 3h (low).
